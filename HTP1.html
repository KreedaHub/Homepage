Tic Tac Toe

Objective

Be the first to get three of your marks (X or O) in a row â€“ horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Game Rules

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Two players take turns placing X and O in empty squares.
  • The first player to get three in a row wins.
  • If all squares are filled and no one has won, the game is a draw.

How to Play

Click on an empty square to place your mark. Players take turns placing X or O. The game ends when a player wins or the grid is full.

Winning Conditions

Win by forming a straight line of three marks (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

Tips & Strategy


  • Try to block your opponent from winning.
  • Take the center square for more chances to win.
  • Plan a two-way win to trap your opponent.
